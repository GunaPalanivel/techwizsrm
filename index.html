About

TECHWIZ is a dynamic and innovative technical club at SRM University, Ramapuram, Chennai, which is built upon a foundation of mutual respect, trust, and support.

Mission

To provide a platform for students to explore and expand their technical skills while fostering a sense of community and collaboration.

Our team is comprised of talented individuals who are experts in their respective fields, bringing a wealth of knowledge and experience to the table. We encourage our members to share their ideas, insights, and experiences, and to challenge themselves to reach their full potential.
